Hello, I'm

Furkan Ercan

I am a recent Ph.D. graduate from Integrated Systems for Information Processing (ISIP) lab at McGill University. My academic advisor was Prof. Warren J. Gross.
Core of my research features practical algorithms and implementations of polar code decoders, a coding scheme that has been recently adopted by the 5G wireless communications standard.
