Hello, I'm

Furkan Ercan.

I am a Postdoctoral Associate at the Wireless Integrated Systems and Extreme Circuits (WISE-Circuits) lab at Boston University, working with Prof. Rabia Tuğçe Yazıcıgil, in collaboration with Prof. Muriel Médard from MIT and Prof. Ken Duffy from Maynooth University. I also serve as the Vice-Chair of IEEE Montreal Section.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Integrated Systems for Information Processing (ISIP) lab at McGill University. Before joining BU, I worked at Octasic Inc as a 5G PHY algorithm developer.


The focus of my postdoctoral research is on the recent emerging universal decoder algorithms and their implementations, for beyond 5G. My Ph.D. research features algorithms and implementations of polar codes, the newest coding scheme of the 5G wireless communications standard.
